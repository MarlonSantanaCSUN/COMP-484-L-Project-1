Titan Classes

Titans are large mech units that provide heavy firepower and protection on the battlefield. Titan classes include Assault, Ogre, Striker, and others, each with unique abilities and weapons. Players can call in Titans during multiplayer matches or the campaign, dramatically changing the dynamics of combat. Choosing the right Titan class affects playstyle, from aggressive front-line combat to defensive support roles.

Customization

Players can customize both their Pilot and Titan loadouts. Options include weapons, abilities, cosmetic skins, and tactical upgrades. Customization allows adaptation to different maps, game modes, and playstyles. It adds depth to multiplayer strategy and personal expression.
