About the Game

Titanfall 2 is a fast-paced first-person shooter developed by Respawn Entertainment. It features fluid parkour movement and giant mech combat known as Titans.

Click to learn more about the campaign

The single-player campaign follows a rifleman named Jack Cooper and his Titan BT-7274. Their relationship becomes one of the most memorable parts of the game.

Titanfall 2 is widely praised for having one of the best FPS campaigns ever created.
